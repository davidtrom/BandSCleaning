Important Information Regarding Covid-19

B & S Cleaning Services is taking the Covid-19 pandemic very seriously. The safety of our staff and clients is of utmost importance to us. It is because of this that in so much as it is possible, B & S Cleaning Services is following all CDC guidelines in regards to social distancing. A link to these guidelines can be found HERE. Our employees will also wear a mask and gloves when cleaning your home or business. We respectfully ask that while we are working inside your home, you also wear a mask for the protection of all parties. We also ask that if you are not feeling well or have any symptoms of Covid-19, please reschedule your appointment and consult your primary healthcare provider for information on being tested.

We are committed to giving you piece of of mind during these uncertain times and thank you for placing your trust in B & S Cleaning Services for all your cleaning needs.
